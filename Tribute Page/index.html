Stan Lee

Marvel's Real Life Superhero
The MCU's greatest superheroes embrace their creator one last time.

    Timeline of an Extraordinary Man

  • 1922 - Born in New York City
  • 1939 - Hired as office assistant for Timely Comics, eventually becoming a writer
  • 1941 - Co-creates first character, the Destroyer
  • 1942 - Joins the Army Signal Corps during World War II, responsible for writing manuals, training films, and occasionally cartooning.
  • 1960 - Creates first comic with Marvel: The Fantasic Four.
  • 1974 - Awarded the Inkport Award
  • 1981 - Moves to LA to focus on Marvel films
  • 1998 - Founds Stan Lee Media
  • 2006 - Hosts the series, "Who Wants to be a Superhero"
  • 2012 - Awarded the Lifetime Achievement Award
  • 2018 - Dies of Pneumonia

“Most people say, “I can’t wait to retire so I can play golf,” or go yachting or whatever they do. Well, if I was playing golf, I would want that to finish so I could go and dream up a new TV show.”

-- Stan Lee
